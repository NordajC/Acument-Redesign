Improve Customer Journey of VIP Guests



Our Client is a world renowned actor in the hospitality industry aiming to provide the best customer experience to their top 1% spenders.

We focused on optimizing the customer journey and enhancing with digital solutions. We provided the whole scrum team consisting of Product Owners, UX Designers, Business Analysts and few Full-Stack developers to conduct user research and develop new mobile applications following best technology practices i.e. Cloud Native, native app iOS/Android development, Agile, DevOps.

New features released included Booking (hotel, concerts, dinner, transportation) and Redemption (Chips,Vouchers) and delivery was on-time and within budget. NPS survey indicates strong customer satisfaction and retention. Usage of Mobile app is high which allows to collect and analyse customer data and design more personalised marketing campaigns.

Transform The organisation into a data-driven culture



Our Client is a world renowned company in the Luxury Retail industry aiming to leverage the power of data to generate more actionable insights to create business value.

We focused on designing and implementing the end-to-end data journey. We provided the whole scrum team consisting of Data architect, Data Engineers, Dashboard Specialists (PowerBI, Tableau, MicroStrategy) and Change Management Project Manager to drive the data transformation from building Data Lake on the Cloud to running Machine Learning models. Dashboards have been released company-wide (HR, Finance, POS) and business stakeholders have been trained to understand and perform analytics to gain a competitive edge regarding their customer behaviour.

The next step is to build a Smart Engine that would provide recommendation to the business for Next Best Action (NBA) and ultimately achieving Marketing Automation capability.

Technology Refreshment and Team Capability Uplifting



Our Client is a HK-based company in the Insurance industry aiming to migrate legacy applications to the Cloud (Microsoft Azure).

We focused on designing the future architecture of the applications to fulfil the expected performance. We turned the applications from monolithic to microservices architecture and upgraded the code from Java 5 to Java 8 / SpringBoot and Node.JS and using Kafka as MQ. We provided a Technical Lead, Cloud Expert, Back-end developers.

The applications have been rewritten with the latest technology and successfully migrated into the Cloud. The performance have been enhanced and the application is more responsive and scalable to better support the business growth. The clients team has been properly upskilled in order to maintain the new application going forward.
