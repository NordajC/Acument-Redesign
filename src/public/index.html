Improve Customer Journey of VIP Guests



Our Client is a world renowned actor in the hospitality industry aiming to provide the best customer experience to their top 1% spenders.

We focused on optimizing the customer journey and enhancing with digital solutions. We provided the whole scrum team consisting of Product Owners, UX Designers, Business Analysts and few Full-Stack developers to conduct user research and develop new mobile applications following best technology practices i.e. Cloud Native, native app iOS/Android development, Agile, DevOps.

New features released included Booking (hotel, concerts, dinner, transportation) and Redemption (Chips,Vouchers) and delivery was on-time and within budget. NPS survey indicates strong customer satisfaction and retention. Usage of Mobile app is high which allows to collect and analyse customer data and design more personalised marketing campaigns.
